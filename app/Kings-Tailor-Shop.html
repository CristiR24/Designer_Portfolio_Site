My Role

Brief

I have been approached with the task of creating a modern landing page for a tailor shop with a rich history behind it. Preserving heritage while designing a contemporary landing page is a must in this project. Also, the goal was to show that wearing tailored clothing is marvelous without directly saying it. I had no restrictions design-wise as long as the client was happy with the result.

Challenges

Making it easy to shop for the conservative and/or busy ones.

I created a non-e-commerce — e-commerce project by getting rid of the sign-ups, virtual shopping carts, and the inserting card data on the website. This was achieved by calling the client to schedule an appointment. Also, the payment is made on the day – cash or credit card.

Preserving heritage while being modern. Standing out.

Using the older visual language, combined with the new school of design I achieved exactly what we needed. The font itself is from the modern serif category. The serifs provide the aged look and the high contrast between thin and thick lines are making it fresh. Also, I used strict squares and playful layouts for the exact same purpose.

Being appealing to the new generation of fashion-heads.

Using a subtle yet very stylish visual language is everything that the millennials need to trust a fashion brand.
PS: Influencers! Influencers! Influencers! Having a trendsetter in the testimonials section definitely helps.

Brand

The Red Dot

Have you ever encountered a basic symbol with more meaning than a dot? Especially if is used as a period. Moreover it is red. King’s Tailor Shop are making a statement, both with words and clothing.

Uncapitalized Letter

The owners last name is King, but if we use it uncapitalized, it brings more royalty to the table. It is quite unique to break the rules in such a niche. Subtle yet bold.
